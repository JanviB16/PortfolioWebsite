What is Foodprint+?

This application will allow users to understand when their
products and foods get spoiled. Millions of foods are getting
wasted every day and it has become a global issue. The
developer has designed an app that could potentially make
users productive and be mindful of when their foods are safe/
fresh to eat and keep track of their freshness. Fresh produce
is getting wasted the most because people do not consume
them as much as processed foods.


The user would have to input all the foods they have prepared/
brought, then its timer would be set into the app. Each time
the food/meal is near its expiry date, the app would alert the
user so they are notified. This will ensure people are eating
right and will prevent them from getting sick and also prevent
food waste

Frequently Asked Questions

How does this app work?

You simply scan your food barcodes/QR codes or grocery store receipts and it inputs all its data into the app. You could also inout your own food into the app if there is nothing to scan. You will recieve a notification or reminder tellling you whetehr or not your food got expired or is close to expiring

Do I need to pay any additional fees

Not at all. This app is totally free!

Is this app compatible for all devices?

Yes. However the developer is currently working on creating the app for desktop and laptop devices.
